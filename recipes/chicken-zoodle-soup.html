Chicken Zoodle Soup

Description

With the winter months approaching fast my niece and I co-conspired to come up with this warm and comforting soup using 'zoodles' that's easy on the waistline.

  • Preparation Time: 20 mins
  • Cook Time: 25 mins
  • Total Time: 45 mins
  • Servings: 6
  • Yield: 12 cups

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 cup diced onion
  • 1 cup diced celery
  • 3 minced cloves garlic
  • 5 (14.5 ounce) cans low-sodium chicken broth
  • 1 cup diced carrot
  • 0.75 pound, cut intro bite sized pieces, cooked chicken breast
  • 0.5 teaspoon dried basil
  • 0.5 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 pinch dried thyme (optional)
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • 3, cut into noodles using a spiral slicer or vegetable peeler, zucchini squash

Steps

  1. Heat olive oil in a large pot over medium-high heat.
    Saute onion, celery, and garlic in hot oil until just tender, about 5 minutes.
  2. Pour chicken broth into the pot; add carrots, chicken, basil, oregano, thyme, salt, and pepper.
    Bring the broth to a boil, reduce heat to medium-low, and simmer mixture until the vegetables are tender, about 20 minutes.
  3. Divide zucchini noodles between six soup bowls; ladle broth mixture over the noodles.
